# Pilot Offer

## Who The Pilot Is For

The first paid pilot is for contractor, specialty trade, and construction back-office teams that already spend meaningful time reviewing certificates of insurance and related compliance paperwork.

The strongest fit is an organization that:

- receives a steady stream of subcontractor or vendor COIs
- currently reviews them through inboxes, shared folders, and spreadsheets
- wants local deployment rather than a cloud-first document workflow
- needs human review and auditability, not black-box automation claims

## Exact First Workflow

The pilot workflow is narrow by design:

1. intake COIs through local upload or staged folder import
2. classify the document and extract practical COI review fields
3. send low-confidence or incomplete results to a human reviewer
4. record approvals, corrections, and follow-up decisions in an audit trail
5. make reviewed records searchable and exportable for operational handoff

This is a compliance review workflow, not a general-purpose document automation rollout.

## What Is Included

- on-prem or customer-managed deployment posture
- web UI for upload, review, search, and admin basics
- COI-focused classification and field extraction workflow
- human review queue with confidence, provenance, and correction support
- audit events for consequential workflow actions
- CSV export of reviewed records

## What Is Intentionally Excluded

- broad "all construction documents" coverage from day one
- deep ERP, accounting, or project-management integrations
- multi-tenant SaaS delivery
- fully automated approval of compliance-sensitive documents
- advanced analytics or executive dashboards
- custom workflow design for every department

Other document families such as permits, lien waivers, invoices, change orders, contracts, and inspection reports remain later expansion paths after the COI workflow proves out.

## What Pilot Success Looks Like

The pilot is successful if the customer can say:

- reviewers spend less time opening and rekeying COIs
- missing or unclear documents are easier to identify and follow up on
- human review decisions are more consistent and easier to explain
- the audit trail is good enough for real operational accountability
- the local deployment model is acceptable to the customer's IT and operations stakeholders

## What The Pilot Should Prove

The pilot should prove commercial credibility, not just technical possibility.

Specifically, it should show that ProjectDoc Local can become a paid system of assisted compliance review for contractor-style businesses before expanding into the broader project-document platform described elsewhere in the repository.
